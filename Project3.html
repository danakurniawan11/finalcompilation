Project 3: Web/Visual Translation

For this project, I wanted to create a timeline around my complicated relationship to design. By allowing myself to finally pursue design now, I’m reflecting on six main events that characterized my current relationship to design. In each event, I’m collating a collection of images and inner monologues that will appear in a horizontal scroll fashion on my webpage.
